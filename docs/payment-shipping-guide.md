# Payment and Shipping Guide

## Cash on Delivery

1. Open Shopify Admin > Settings > Payments.
2. Add a manual payment method.
3. Name it Cash on Delivery.
4. Add instructions: "Our team may confirm your order by phone or WhatsApp before dispatch."
5. Test checkout.

## Bank Transfer

1. Add a manual payment method.
2. Enter bank name, account title, account number or IBAN.
3. Ask customers to share proof of payment by WhatsApp or email.

## JazzCash and Easypaisa

If you do not have a gateway yet, add each as a manual payment method:

1. Method name: JazzCash Manual Payment or Easypaisa Manual Payment.
2. Add account number and account title.
3. Ask customers to send transaction ID or screenshot.

## Shipping across Pakistan

1. Open Settings > Shipping and delivery.
2. Create a Pakistan shipping zone.
3. Add flat rates by order value, city, or weight.
4. Add free shipping above your chosen threshold.
5. Test checkout with addresses in major cities and remote areas.

## Future payment gateway

Later, connect a supported payment provider for Pakistan from Shopify Payments settings or third-party payment providers. Test thoroughly before launch.
